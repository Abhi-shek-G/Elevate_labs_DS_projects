Optimization Recommendations: Healthcare Appointment No-Show Reduction

1. Dynamic Scheduling: Prioritize same week scheduling to minimize 'WaitDays', which is the primary predictor of no-shows.
2. Targeted SMS: Increase reminders for appointments booked more than 7 days in advance.
3. Demographic Focus: Roll out digital calendar integrations for the 18-35 age group.
4. Strategic Overbooking: Overbook appointments classified as 'High Risk' by the prediction model.
5. Confirmation Loops: Enforce a 48-hour re-confirmation for long-lead bookings to release unused slots.
